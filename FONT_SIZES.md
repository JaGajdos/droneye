# Zoznam veľkostí fontov v CSS

## Veľkosti v rem jednotkách:

### Veľké nadpisy:
- **3.5rem** - Hero title (desktop)
- **3rem** - Hero title (desktop), Section title (desktop)
- **2.5rem** - Section title (desktop), Hero title (tablet)
- **2rem** - Section title (tablet), Carousel title, Process step number, Pricing title
- **1.8rem** - Equipment card title
- **1.75rem** - Section title (mobile)

### Stredné nadpisy:
- **1.5rem** - Button text, Card title, Equipment card title, Process step title, Pricing card title, Footer section title
- **1.3rem** - Hero subtitle (desktop), Equipment card feature, Pricing card title, Postproduction step number
- **1.25rem** - Mobile menu link
- **1.2rem** - Hero subtitle, Section subtitle, Theme button, Video title, Photo title, Carousel tab, Mobile menu link
- **1.1rem** - Section text, Card text, Button text, Video title, Photo title, Equipment card specs, Process step description, Pricing card text

### Normálny text:
- **1rem** - Body text, Button text, Card text, Form input, Footer text, Equipment card specs, Process step description, Pricing card text
- **0.95rem** - Team card description, Pricing card text
- **0.9rem** - Theme option, Footer link, Equipment card specs, Process step description, Pricing card text
- **0.85rem** - Language option, Process step description, Pricing card text
- **0.8rem** - Equipment card specs (mobile)
- **0.75rem** - Process step description (mobile)

## Veľkosti v px jednotkách:

### Veľké:
- **40px** - Lightbox close button
- **30px** - Lightbox navigation buttons, Donut chart label (desktop)
- **24px** - Lightbox navigation buttons (tablet)
- **20px** - Donut chart center text
- **16px** - Donut chart segment label
- **14px** - Donut chart label (tablet), Donut chart segment label (tablet)
- **13px** - Donut chart segment label (tablet)
- **12px** - Donut chart segment label (mobile)
- **11px** - Donut chart segment label (mobile)

## Zhrnutie podľa kategórií:

### Hlavné nadpisy (Hero, Section):
- 3.5rem, 3rem, 2.5rem, 2rem, 1.75rem

### Podnadpisy:
- 1.3rem, 1.2rem, 1.1rem

### Tlačidlá:
- 1.5rem, 1.2rem, 1.1rem, 1rem, 0.85rem

### Telo textu:
- 1.1rem, 1rem, 0.95rem, 0.9rem, 0.85rem, 0.8rem, 0.75rem

### Menu a navigácia:
- 1.25rem, 1.2rem, 1.1rem, 0.9rem, 0.85rem

### Špeciálne prvky (Lightbox, Donut chart):
- 40px, 30px, 24px, 20px, 16px, 14px, 13px, 12px, 11px

## Poznámky:
- Väčšina textu používa rem jednotky pre lepšiu škálovateľnosť
- px jednotky sa používajú len pre špeciálne prvky (lightbox, donut chart)
- Najmenšia veľkosť: 0.75rem (11px ekvivalent)
- Najväčšia veľkosť: 3.5rem (56px ekvivalent pri 16px base)
